Who We Are

Evince Consulting is a dynamic research & development consultancy firm with offices in Nairobi, Kenya and Mogadishu, Somalia. We bridge institutional capacity gaps and support organizations to deliver sustainable impact through tailored, high-quality services.

Our team comprises experienced humanitarian and development specialists supported by local enumerators across Kenya and Somalia.

We prioritize client vision, integrity, cultural respect, continuous improvement, and excellence in all engagements.
